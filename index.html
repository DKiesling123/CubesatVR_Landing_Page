What is the CubeSat VR Program?

The CubeSat VR Program is a cutting-edge initiative that combines the power of miniature satellites (CubeSats) with virtual reality technology. Our goal is to bring space data to life—empowering engineers, researchers, and enthusiasts with immersive insights into orbits, trajectories, and onboard systems.

What Can It Do?

Real-Time Simulation

Experience your CubeSat’s orbit and positioning in real time using our immersive VR environment, complete with accurate tracking and live data feeds.

Mission Planning

Easily plan, design, and test missions using interactive 3D models and data visualization tools, helping you prototype your vision before ever leaving Earth.

Training & Education

Engage students, scientists, and the public with simplified orbital mechanics lessons in VR. Perfect for classrooms and outreach events.

Collaboration Tools

Work seamlessly with teams around the world in real-time shared VR sessions. Share data, brainstorm ideas, and troubleshoot from any location.

Why We Need CubeSat VR

Space missions are increasingly complex. By harnessing VR for mission planning, data visualization, and education, the CubeSat VR Program aims to advance human knowledge while saving resources and engaging the next generation of scientists.

  • Cut costs through virtual testing & prototyping
  • Visualize orbital data for efficient mission design
  • Collaborate remotely with international partners in a shared VR workspace
  • Inspire the innovators of tomorrow
